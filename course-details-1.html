About

Welcome to the Abhiprerna Foundation's NCR Blanket Distribution Drive (NBDD), a compassionate initiative aimed at providing warmth and comfort to vulnerable individuals enduring harsh winter nights in the National Capital Region. Our mission is to ensure that no one faces the bitter cold alone, and through NBDD, we have distributed over 8,000 blankets to those battling the elements. In the heart of winter, our dedicated team works tirelessly to bring warmth and survival to those in need. The NBDD is not just a distribution drive; it is a symbol of collective kindness that transcends socio-economic boundaries. Every blanket distributed is a testament to the power of community and compassion. As we continue our efforts to combat the cold waves, we invite you to join hands with us in making a difference. Your support can help us extend the reach of NBDD and provide solace to more individuals during these challenging times. Together, let's create a winter where no one is left out in the cold. Join Abhiprerna Foundation in spreading warmth and love through the NCR Blanket Distribution Drive
