Vermi Compost Formation

Vermicompost is the product of the decomposition process using various species of worms, usually red wigglers, white worms, and other earthworms, to create a mixture of decomposing vegetable or food waste, bedding materials, and vermicast. Vermicast is the end-product of the breakdown of organic matter by earthworms.

Why Organic Farming?

Organic farming focuses on cultivating crops without synthetic chemicals, relying on natural methods like crop rotation and composting. It promotes soil health, biodiversity, and environmental sustainability, offering healthier food options while minimizing ecological impact and supporting local ecosystems.
