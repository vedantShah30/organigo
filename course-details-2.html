About

The Smile Foundation with its headquarters in Chennai is a lighthouse of compassion and goodwill, unwaveringly devoted to the mission of fighting hunger among the disadvantaged. They bring a line of life to those suffering from food insecurity through their carefully planned food distribution programs. The underlying foundation of this noble task is the unshakable conviction that no one should ever go through the pangs of hunger when surrounded by plenty. Generous donations, though mainly from kindhearted people, are the backbone of their operations, allowing them to buy nutritional foods and deliver it to the needy. No matter what the condition, the foundation works to ensure that every stomach is full and every heart is filled with hope. They thus not only give food to relieve hunger, but they also plant the seed for an even better, more promising future for the communities they are serving. Through relentless hard work, they epitomize the spirit of humanity and solidarity, having an unforgettable presence in the lives they affect.
