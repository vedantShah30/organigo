About

Nestled in the sacred city of Varanasi, Shivalaay Old Age Home extends a warm embrace to its residents. Lately, our family has grown significantly, and with that, our needs have multiplied. We find ourselves in urgent need of essentials like medicine, beds, and kitchen utensils to ensure the well-being and comfort of our beloved residents. But fret not, for we believe in the power of community and collective support. We humbly appeal to your generosity to help us raise the necessary funds. Together, let's create a haven where our elderly can find solace and care in their golden years. Your contribution, no matter how big or small, will make a world of difference in shaping a comfortable future for those who have walked the longest miles of life's journey. Join us in spreading love and compassion to those who have paved the way for generations to come.
